SEOUL ANIMATION CENTER

Seoul Animation Center is a culture complex space in downtown Seoul divided into the Comics Cafe and Ani Picnic. As a library housing an endless source of comics for comic fanatics out there, the first-floor Comics Cafe offers visitors various contentfrom over 30,000 of the latest comics from home and abroad, from recent releases to more nostalgic comic series from way back in the 90s. The second-floor Ani Picnic is a experiential zone where visitors can meet popular characters from Korean comicseries customized to a theme of traveling in Seoul. Some of the featured characters included are from popular series such as Pororo, Super Wings, Turning Mecard, Slow Slow Sloth Neul, and Alice in Wonderland, all captured at different areas around thecity be it Seoul Station, Namsan, Gwanghwamun, or even Seoul Forest!
